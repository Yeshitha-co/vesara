```bash

```

Then:
- Create new repo at github.com
- Follow GitHub's instructions to push

### Step 2: Connect to Vercel
1. Go to vercel.com
2. Sign up with GitHub
3. Click "New Project"
4. Import your GitHub repo
5. Click "Deploy"

### Step 3: Share Your URL!
Your website will be LIVE at:
